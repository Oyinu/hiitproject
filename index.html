About Us

HIIT PLC is a leading provider of IT training and consulting services in Nigeria, dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the digital age. With over 25 years of experience, HiiT Plc has established itself as a trusted name in the IT education sector.

At HiiT Plc, we offer a wide range of industry-recognized IT courses, covering areas such as software development, networking, cybersecurity, data science, and more. Our courses are designed to meet the evolving needs of the IT industry, ensuring that our students are equipped with the latest skills and knowledge.

Our team of experienced instructors are industry professionals who bring real-world expertise to the classroom. They are passionate about teaching and are committed to helping students succeed in their careers.

In addition to our training programs, HiiT Plc also offers consulting services to help businesses leverage technology for growth and innovation. Our consultants work closely with clients to understand their unique challenges and develop tailored solutions that drive results.

Whether you are an individual looking to enhance your IT skills or an organization seeking expert consulting services, HiiT Plc is here to help you achieve your goals. Join us today and take the first step towards a successful career in IT.
